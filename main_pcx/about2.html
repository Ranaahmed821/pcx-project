PCX Computer
Since ancient times, the symbol X has been used to indicate the unknown, and so have some users searching for personal computers for use in various fields, but their lack of knowledge and ignorance of all the details of computer parts and ways to compatible them together was an obstacle to them relying on themselves, and The idea for PCX came from developers who faced the same problem and The solution was Firstly, to provide a website that sells computer spare parts separately In a simple, easy and smooth way without complexity Secondly, by provide the "build your own bundle" and "PCX engine " service...The first is for those who have sufficient experience to build an personal computer through one page without the need to move between more than one link, page or tab...The second is equivalent to a seller with experience and knowledge in the field of computers, where the customer only asks about the budget and the category in which he wants to use the PC, In all services, the customer ends up with an invoice for an PC build to meet his personal needs in his field within his own budget, and he has the option of purchasing the same device from another site or from "PCX website".
